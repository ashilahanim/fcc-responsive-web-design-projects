The Tribute Page for Prophet Muhammad p.b.u.h

Prophet Muhammad was born around a thousand years ago, and his teachings based on the Al-Quran remain strong until this day.

This page is created to remind us of the last prophet, Muhammad bin Abdullah p.b.u.h. This page will tell us the brief history of his early life, prophecy era, and his ways of life.

Brief History of Prophet Muhammad p.b.u.h

  • 570 CE - Born in Makkah, part of the Quraysh tribe.
  • 576 CE - His mother Siti Aminah passed away; raised by his grandfather.
  • 578 CE - His grandfather Abdul Mutalib passed away; uncle Abu Talib cared for him.
  • 595 CE - Married Siti Khadijah, a kind and wealthy merchant.
  • 610 CE - Received first revelation from Jibril a.s (also known as Gabriel) in the Cave of Hira'.
  • 613 CE - Publicly preached Islam; faced persecution from Quraysh leaders.
  • 622 CE - Migrated (Hijrah) to Madinah (Medina), marking the Islamic calendar's beginning.
  • 624-628 CE - Led major battles and signed the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah with the Quraysh.
  • 630 CE - Conquered Makkah peacefully; purified the Kaabah from idols worship.
  • 632 CE - Passed away in Madinah 9Medina) after delivering his Farewell Sermon (Khutbah).
